APAR Industries manufactures a comprehensive range of cables including power cables, control cables, instrument cables, specialty cables, solar cables, submersible cables, welding cables, and winding wires. Our products cater to diverse industries including power generation, transmission, distribution, oil & gas, renewable energy, and infrastructure projects.

APAR offers industry-leading warranty periods across our product range. Cables come with a standard warranty of 12-18 months from the date of dispatch, while our transformer oils and industrial lubricants come with a 24-month warranty when stored under recommended conditions. Extended warranty options are available for bulk and long-term projects. Please contact our customer service for specific warranty details.
